Fast launches are not about picking trendy tools. They are about reducing unknowns.

## Use this decision order

1. Team familiarity
2. Delivery speed for core features
3. Operational simplicity
4. Flexibility for iteration

## Common mistake

Many teams over-design architecture before validating demand. That slows learning and increases cost.

## Better approach

Choose a stack that your team can ship with confidently in weeks, not months. Then harden the system after meaningful adoption signals.

The best stack is the one that helps you learn fastest with acceptable risk.
